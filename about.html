Be Part of Our Community

Chicago sits on the edge of Lake Michigan with beautiful views of the water that families, students, and tourists come to to enjoy everyday especially during the summer. The Loop and the North river neighborhoods are the densest and busiest neighborhoods in the city. While these neighborhood provide beaches and parks for citizens to enjoy the Lake, there are no opportunities to go out on the water from these areas. People have to travel up north past Lincoln Park or west to the river to find a watersports facility.
