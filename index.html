Best Surfing

We as young adults notice that there is no possibility to do fun watersports near the center of Chicago. We would provide an easy, quick and affordable rental service for water sports including but not limited to jet surf, jet skis and water trampolines. It would be open during the summer for all to enjoy and a great spot for tourists to try something exciting!

Why Fun Factory?

The Fun Factory Chicago is a watersports facility located on Ohio Beach in Chicago, IL. We will be renting watersports equipment such as Jet Surfs, Jet Skis, Stand-Up-Paddle boards, Water Walking Ball, Kayaks, Water Trampoline, Skim-Board, Sea scooter (more)

Newsletter

The overall goal is to establish a new Watersport Center at Ohio Beach right next to Navy Pier. This will bring more seasonal fun along the lake in the downtown area, especially for kids and people who are searching for a different experience in Chicago. The location and proximity to Navy Pier, provides ideal access to both locals having a day downtown and tourists visiting Chicago. To advertise our new business, we look to social media such as Facebook and youtube, and even have our own website.
