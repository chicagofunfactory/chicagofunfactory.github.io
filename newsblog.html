  • Oct 2016

    Why Chicago Fun Factory?

    There will be many benefits from our business. It will get people to be more active and engage in a healthy lifestyle. A locally sourced cafe will help support the watersports center and encourage sustainability in the area. The Fun Factory Chicago will also make downtown a more attractive place to be by adding a desirable activity to the area. This will bring more people to downtown including students who are out target market. We will be provide discounts for students as initiative to visit us and invigorate the area.

  • Nov 2016

    Who?

    Our target customer base will be focused on kids and sporty families and students. Students in particular as there are many universities in the area to draw a customer base from. We will be targeting from 10 through 45 years of age (roughly 1.6 million people in Chicago; 57% from population). Water sport are great for families to enjoy together as well and we would like to get them involved.
